Dr. Ashish Agarwal

Dr. Ashish Agarwal, A notable name in Delhi's Cardiology scene. He is the Director and Senior Consultant Cardiologist at AKASH SUPER SPECIALITY HOSPITAL, Dwarka, New Delhi, India. He is highly skilled to handle complex cardiac cases and their management.

He is well-known among his patients and their peers for his outstanding care and empathetic approach.

Owing to his expertise he is currently the Director and HOD of the Cardiology department at Akash Hospital.

Dr. Ashish Agarwal is an alumnus of renowned institutions in India. He has wide experience of over 15 years and has treated both in-patient and emergency cases skillfully. He is associated with over 21 National and International publications owing to his capability as an adept cardiologist.
